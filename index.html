The Birth of Ittehad

THE BIRTH OF ITTEHAD

Founded in 2023, Ittehad Organization was born from a simple but powerful belief: those overlooked by society deserve not just attention, but equality, dignity, and opportunity. What started as an idea quickly turned into a movement, one that actively works to uplift voices often left unheard.

From empowering education through Project Aaftab, to driving real change with environmental cleaning campaigns alongside COMSATS University Islamabad, to building platforms for dialogue and leadership through three successful editions of Ittehad MUN, every initiative reflects our commitment to unity in action. At Ittehad, we don’t just believe in change, we create it, together.

OUR PURPOSE

Ittehad is structured around action, inclusion, and sustained impact. Each project exists to turn values into visible results, whether through education, community service, or diplomatic leadership.

EQUALITY IN ACTION

Providing spaces where unheard voices can participate and be taken seriously.

LEADERSHIP THROUGH SERVICES

Building a community that learns by doing and grows through responsibility.

Our Founder

FOUNDER'S VISION

Introducing the Founder of Ittehad Organization, Ayesha A. Dawood, a distinguished scholar of Law and International Relations. She stands as the visionary force behind Ittehad, the architect of its ideals, and the unwavering pillar upon which each of its endeavors is built.
